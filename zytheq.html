Home / Process

A structured search from intake to interview.

Zytheq combines career positioning, search operations, recruiter outreach, interview preparation, and mentorship into one coordinated program.

Timeline

How the program moves.

Step 01

Discovery and fit

We review your background, authorization, experience level, target roles, salary expectations, and mentorship availability.

Output: program fit decision and search direction.

Step 02

Positioning and assets

We rebuild your resume variants, LinkedIn profile, target company list, and baseline outreach language.

Output: recruiter-ready materials and role-specific messaging.

Step 03

Applications and outreach

We run a tracked campaign across target roles, recruiters, hiring managers, and warm paths where available.

Output: weekly visibility, follow-ups, and active pipeline management.

Step 04

Mentorship and impact

You mentor an Indian student four to five hours per week through structured sessions and practical career conversations.

Output: measurable student support without inventing a curriculum from scratch.

Step 05

Interview and close

We help you prepare company briefs, role narratives, behavioral stories, technical examples, and negotiation readiness.

Output: clearer conversations and stronger interview conversion.
